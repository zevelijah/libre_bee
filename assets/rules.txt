Game Rules:
You are provided seven distict letters, including one you must use. You are to enter words, subject to the following constraints:
1. You may only use the provided letters, though you may use each as many times as you desire.
2. You must use the required letter at least once in your word.
3. Only english words as recognized by Josh Wardle's powerlanguage word list, available on github. Thanks, Josh.
4. Must be at least four letters long.
5. You may not enter the same letter multiple times.

Scoring is based on this system:
1. 4-letter words score one point.
2. Longer words score one word per letter.
3. Words using all seven letters earn a 7-point bonus.
