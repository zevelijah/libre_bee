Commands:
/help - combines /rules, /commands, and /info
/rules - Only shows game rules
/commands - only shows game commands
/info - shows creator and liscense information

/shuffle - Move the letters around in the display

/license - full GNU 3.0 license
/warranty - Shows GNU Warranty Message

/found - Shows words you've discovered so far

/quit - shows you game score and words used and exits the program
